Our Story

Neo Service Layer was born from the recognition that blockchain development remained unnecessarily complex, limiting innovation and adoption. Our founding team, comprised of blockchain veterans and distributed systems experts, saw an opportunity to change this.

We started with a simple question: What if developers could build on blockchain as easily as they build traditional applications? This led us to create a comprehensive service layer that handles the complexity while providing the power and flexibility developers need.

Today, Neo Service Layer powers applications across DeFi, gaming, supply chain, and more. We're proud to be at the forefront of making blockchain technology accessible to developers worldwide.
